## The approach

**Bellerophon GemInclusion** is a personal reference base dedicated to the photomicrography of inclusions in gemstones.

Each card is built from direct observations through a gemological microscope, with identification rigour inspired by major laboratory methods (GIA, Gübelin, SSEF).

## Methodology

Photomicrographs are captured with a trinocular microscope in both darkfield and brightfield. Magnifications range from 10× (overview) to 100× (detail of included crystals).

Cards systematically document:

- The **host gem** and its gemological variety
- The **inclusion type** identified (mineral, fluid, healed fracture, growth)
- The **geographic origin** when determined
- **Treatments detected or absent** — a key factor for appraisal
- The **magnification** used during capture
