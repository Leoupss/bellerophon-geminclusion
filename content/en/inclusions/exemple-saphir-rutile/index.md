"Silk" consists of fine rutile needles oriented along corundum's crystallographic axes. Under reflected light they scatter a characteristic silky sheen. This specimen is from an alluvial deposit in Sri Lanka (Ratnapura) and shows three intersecting orientations at 60°, typical of the species.
